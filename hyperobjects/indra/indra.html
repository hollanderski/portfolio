Indra's net : dream about a new social network

The web contains in its structure a new spatio-temporal model. In particular, it replaces the hierarchical organization of information by a spatializing hypertext, sequential storage by a storage random access and the psychological movement of storytelling by the psychological movement across space. The best image for the web is a graph. With node servers and edge connections, the web indexes its data by producing metadata. Far from being chaotic, these data are organized in an ecosystem way. Man exudes data, which self-regulates in clusters.
Topological proximity is correlated with semantic similarity. There is a match between the content and the structure of the web.The hyperlink brings the reader from one text to another, endlessly. This metonymic system is deployed laterally, rather than vertically.







In Indra's net, data, by producing meaning, is negentropic. Indra’s net is a hermeneutic platform which is more focused on the content of discussions than on individuals, allowing the constitution of groups of interpretations or affinities. The construction of collective meaning takes precedence over individual interests.
An interface directly exposing the graph structure of the data to users could highlight associations of ideas, to produce collective meanings by allowing users to become an observers of thoughts.
Like an interactive, multi-user map, the discussions are linked according to their semantic proximity. Grouping by cluster is done semi-automatically because it is the users who build their common data index using keywords,then the algorithm calculates the weight of the nodes accordingly to users' annotations.
A new representation of time and space using 3D must keep conventional space-time references to allow the user to adapt,like a semiotic index to indicate the direction of reading. A Temporal Data Skeleton may become the successor to the horizontal timeline of cinema and the vertical timeline of papyrus.
The symbolism of Indra's net comes from a Buddhist concept I discovered in the book Gödel, Escher, Bach from Douglas Hofstadter. Indra's network denotes the interconnection and the emptiness of the Universe. All Indra diamonds reflect each other endlessly. This is a good metaphor for a social network, which should ideally portray the holistic nature of the world.
