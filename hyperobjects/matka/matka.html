Tadeusz Rozewicz 100th anniversary


Tadeusz Rozewicz (1921-2014) was a polish poet originary from Radomsko, Poland. For the 100th anniversary of his birth, I did a performance on poetry and music in collaboration with the violonist Tomasz Sroczynski. The performance took place at the Rozewicz Open Festival in Radomsko.





Developed with three.js and ml5.js, 3D visuals react to the pitch and energy of the sound. Animation evolves with the semantic of the poem "Matka ochodzi".
